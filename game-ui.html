HHC Life – Brand Design

A full identity system for a modern cannabis lifestyle brand.

Challenge

HHC Life needed a complete brand identity that would stand out in the competitive cannabis market while communicating trust, innovation, and lifestyle appeal. The challenge was to design a system flexible enough for packaging, apparel, and digital platforms, but consistent enough to be instantly recognizable.

Process

  • Research & Strategy – explored cannabis + lifestyle branding, color psychology, and market differentiation.
  • Logo Development – merged the geometry of a honeycomb with the form of a cannabis leaf.
  • Packaging Design – created a dark premium look with strong orange accents across flower, edibles, and extracts.
  • Merchandising – extended the identity into apparel and brand patterns to build lifestyle recognition.

Outcome

The result was a bold, scalable identity system that works across packaging, print, and digital touchpoints. HHC Life’s products now stand out with a consistent, premium look that appeals to both lifestyle consumers and the cannabis community.
